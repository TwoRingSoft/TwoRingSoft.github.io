Two Ring Software exists to create tools that help people.



Software should work within a dharmic model of interaction:
  • Quality: deliver software that is safe and maintainable–that means code that is tested, well-factored and documented
  • Harmony: we may not be the first or last to touch a codebase–respect the decisions of those that have come before, and try to leave it better than we found it for those who come after
  • Duty: our products are meant to help those who use them–seek to understand users' problems and empathize with their needs
  • Morality: cultivate respect and compassion between...
    1. developers: leave ego behind when solving problems and teaching others
    2. developers and users: shun psychological tricks and exploitative practices
    3. users (and even nonusers): encourage healthy lifestyles and maintain humanity
Above all, we strive for simplicity and elegance in every aspect of our products, whether in code or user experience. {% include closing-tags.html %}
